Our Publishing Services

Journal Publishing

Creating authoritative platforms for academic communities. We provide comprehensive journal publishing solutions for academic associations, research institutions, and editorial boards. From launching new journals to supporting the continued development of established ones, we offer professional support at every stage.

  • Comprehensive Publishing Management: Submission systems, peer-review coordination, editorial production, global distribution.
  • Digitalization and Dissemination: High-quality online publishing platforms, search engine optimization, social media promotion.
  • Open Access Options: Open access publishing models that meet funder requirements.
  • Revenue Management and Growth: Professional subscription management and marketing to ensure long-term sustainability.

Book Publishing

Transform your monograph into a milestone in your field. We publish academic monographs, edited collections, textbooks, and conference proceedings in social sciences, literature, and medicine. We work closely with authors to meticulously craft each work.

  • Professional Editing and Production: Including content development, copyediting, high-quality typesetting, and cover design.
  • Global Distribution Network: Through our partners, your work will be available on Amazon, Barnes & Noble, and major library acquisition platforms worldwide.
  • Targeted Marketing: Developing personalized marketing plans, including book reviews, conference presentations, and direct mail campaigns.
  • Rights Management: Assistance with translation rights and other subsidiary rights to expand your work's international influence.
